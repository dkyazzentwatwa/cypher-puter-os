## Overview

Cryptid Park Ranger is about reading the scene, managing risk, gathering ethical proof, updating a field guide, and handling reports from the park.

## Goal Of A Run

Build proof and resolve reports without losing stamina, supplies, trust, ethics, or safety. The best outcomes come from documenting carefully rather than exploiting every sighting.

## Screen Guide

### STATION

STATION plans reports such as camper, fence, lights, tracks, ridge, and lake leads.

### ZONES

ZONES sends patrols through forest, lake, ridge, cave, camp, road, bog, summit, lodge, and preserve areas.

### GEAR

GEAR packs camera, bait, recorder, sample kit, thermal, medkit, rope, and battery.

### TRACK

TRACK handles print, photo, audio, sample, and sighting evidence decisions.

### GUIDE

GUIDE reviews cryptids such as mossback, lake wisp, ridge antler, cave mute, ash cat, bog lamp, summit moth, and pine crown.

### REPORTS

REPORTS resolves missing camper, fence break, odd lights, injured animal, sponsor, and research chains.

### EVENTS

EVENTS covers clue, weather, rescue, ethics, sponsor, rare, camp, and night situations.

## Core Loop

1. Read reports at STATION.
2. Choose a zone.
3. Pack gear.
4. Patrol and respond to events.
5. Track evidence ethically.
6. Update the guide and reports.

## Risk, Progress, And Endings

Proof helps good endings, but fear, exposure, injury, and unethical choices can damage the park. Endings include science guide, park guardian, town legend, mystery solved, ethics breach, and lost patrol.

## Tips For First Run

- Carry medkit or battery before risky zones.
- Retreat is sometimes the right choice.
- Evidence is better when fear and exposure are controlled.
- Reports can be more urgent than rare sightings.
- Do not trade ethics for quick proof unless you accept the ending risk.

## Offline Fictional Scope

Evidence, reports, radio status, cryptids, and field work are fictional game systems. Cryptid Ranger does not transmit, receive, scan, use GPS, track wildlife, connect to networks, or provide real field guidance.
