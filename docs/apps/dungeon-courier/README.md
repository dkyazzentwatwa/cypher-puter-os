## Player Manual


Genre: fantasy contract delivery RPG.

## Overview

Dungeon Courier is about getting strange parcels across dangerous routes. You accept jobs, pack supplies, pick routes, protect package integrity, and decide whether curiosity is worth the risk.

## Goal Of A Run

Build a trusted courier career before stress, wounds, suspicion, or ruined parcels end the run. Clean deliveries are safest. Weird package choices can unlock stronger story paths, but they also raise risk.

## Controls

Use number keys for visible choices. Use `W/S`, `K/J`, or `;/.` to move. Use `A/D` to change pages. Press `Enter` or BtnA to select. Press `Q`, Backspace, Delete, or Tab to back out.

| `Gold` | Money for supplies, tolls, and recovery. |

## Screen Guide

### TOWN

TOWN sets local mood and services. Visit towns to stabilize before taking harder contracts.

### BOARD

BOARD lists contracts. Each contract represents a parcel, destination, reward, deadline, and risk profile. Accepting a job starts the delivery loop.

### PACK

PACK prepares food, torches, bandages, charms, rope, seals, maps, and ink. Better supplies reduce route losses but cost gold.

### ROUTE

ROUTE handles travel choices. Safe paths are slower but gentler. Common paths balance time and danger. Shortcuts push progress and risk.

### EVENT

EVENT presents hazards, rivals, tolls, weather, cursed packages, and signature trouble. Choices affect package integrity, suspicion, wounds, stamps, and stress.

### DELIVERY

DELIVERY closes the job. Clean, late, damaged, opened, fake-seal, bonus, rival, and secret outcomes all change pay and reputation pressure.

### PERKS

PERKS spends stamps on courier advantages such as route memory, steady hands, silver tongue, medic, lucky stamp, night eye, hard boots, ledger skill, calm, and shortcut sense.

## Core Loop

1. Visit TOWN and read local pressure.
2. Pick a contract from BOARD.
3. Use PACK to prepare.
4. Choose routes and resolve events.
5. Deliver the parcel.
6. Spend stamps on perks.

## Risk, Progress, And Endings

Progress comes from successful jobs and contract chains. Package integrity controls pay and trust. Suspicion and wounds push failure. Endings include trusted career, cursed legend, guild favorite, rival ruin, lost parcel, and opened box paths.

## Tips For First Run

- Pack food and bandages before risky routes.
- Do not open every parcel. Save that for a weird-package run.
- Suspicion is hard to erase once it rises.
- Stamina matters more than speed early.
- Perks compound over the run; buy them before difficulty spikes.
