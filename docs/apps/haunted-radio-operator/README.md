Genre: fictional station mystery.

## Overview

Haunted Radio Operator is a night-shift mystery about managing a lonely station, tuning fictional bands, decoding fake transmissions, tracking cases, reading logs, and routing power under pressure.

## Goal Of A Run

Solve enough station mysteries before power, calm, trust, static, entity pressure, or station condition collapses. The best runs balance curiosity with survival.

## Controls

Use `1` through `5` for actions. Move with `W/S`, `K/J`, or `;/.`. Page with `A/D`. Select with `Enter` or BtnA. Back out with `Q` or Backspace.

## Screen Guide

### STATION

STATION begins each shift and shows the overall condition. Use it to decide whether to tune, decode, review, log, or reroute power.

### TUNE

TUNE selects fictional bands such as Low Band, Ridge, Lake, Numbers, and Dawn. These are game table keys, not real frequencies.

### SIGNAL

SIGNAL presents callers, mimics, number sequences, weather events, hidden transmissions, rangers, child voices, and archivist threads. Choose how to answer.

### DECODE

DECODE uses fictional tools: callsign, reverse, grid, tapcode, weather log, and replay. Decode actions spend power and calm to reveal clues.

### CASES

CASES reviews hiker, mill, camp, lake voice, tunnel, numbers, black pine, and dawn band mysteries.

### LOGS

LOGS stores callsigns, tapes, maps, contradictions, tells, and weather notes discovered through play.

### POWER

POWER reroutes antenna, recorder, heater, floodlight, beacon, and battery systems.

## Core Loop

1. Start the shift at STATION.
2. Tune a fictional band.
3. Answer or ignore signals.
4. Decode when the clue is worth the cost.
5. Review cases and logs.
6. Reroute power before collapse.

## Risk, Progress, And Endings

Low power, high static, low calm, and high entity pressure are dangerous. Clues and trust help solve cases. Endings include dawn operator, vanished shift, truth broadcast, quiet coverup, signal friend, and tower dark.

## Tips For First Run

- Keep power above the danger zone.
- Do not decode every signal immediately.
- Review cases when clues rise.
- Static is a warning, not just flavor.
- Trust can help stabilize better endings.

## Offline Fictional Scope

All frequencies, bands, signals, callers, tuning, and decoding are fictional offline table mechanics. Haunted Radio Operator does not transmit, receive, scan, connect to Wi-Fi, use Bluetooth, access the internet, or listen to real radio.
