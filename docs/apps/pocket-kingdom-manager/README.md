Genre: seasonal kingdom ledger sim.

## Overview

Pocket Kingdom is a ruler's desk squeezed onto the Cardputer. You assign workers, build structures, pass laws, listen to advisors, answer crises, and guide the realm through yearly reports.

## Goal Of A Run

Build a stable dynasty before threat, low morale, shortage, or crisis chains overwhelm the realm. Strong kingdoms balance food, gold, wood, population, soldiers, morale, faith, and threat.

## Controls

Use visible number keys to choose actions. Use `W/S`, `K/J`, or `;/.` to move. Use `A/D` for pages. Press `Enter` or BtnA to select. Use `Q` or Backspace to back out.

## Screen Guide

### REALM

REALM reviews seasonal pressure. Spring, summer, autumn, winter, storms, fairs, and quiet seasons all shift priorities.

### WORK

WORK assigns labor to farms, mines, walls, temples, markets, study, and army. Worker choices shape resource production.

### BUILD

BUILD upgrades farm, granary, barracks, wall, market, shrine, library, tower, roads, and infirmary. Buildings spend gold and wood but create long-term stability.

### LAW

LAW passes decrees such as tax, bread, militia, tithe, trade, school, curfew, water, inheritance, charter, magic, and relief. Laws shift resources, morale, faith, and threat.

### COURT

COURT lets you hear advisors such as steward, marshal, priest, mage, noble, farmer, heir, envoy, judge, and scribe. Advisors help, but their priorities conflict.

### EVENT

EVENT handles famine, raid, plague, scandal, petition, festival, succession, and magic leak crises.

### YEAR

YEAR reports close out major cycles and point toward endings.

## Core Loop

1. Read REALM to see the season.
2. Assign WORK to address shortages.
3. BUILD structures for permanent advantages.
4. Use LAW and COURT to shape politics.
5. Answer crises and review YEAR reports.

## Risk, Progress, And Endings

Progress increases as the dynasty stabilizes. High threat, low morale, and weak resources push collapse. Endings include golden age, iron crown, holy mandate, wizard pact, people rule, dragon treaty, and broken realm.

## Tips For First Run

- Build food and defense before chasing fancy laws.
- Keep morale from falling too far.
- Soldiers help raids, but population and food keep the realm alive.
- Advisor choices are tradeoffs, not free bonuses.
- Threat should be reduced before year-end pressure spikes.
