## Overview

Star Trail Rancher is about moving creatures between planets while keeping habitats, supplies, hull, morale, and ethics under control.

## Goal Of A Run

Complete contracts and discoveries without losing cargo condition, ship stability, or reputation. Ethical deliveries pay less sometimes, but they create safer endings.

## Controls

Use number keys for choices. Move with `W/S`, `K/J`, or `;/.`. Page with `A/D`. Select with `Enter` or BtnA. Back out with `Q` or Backspace.

## Screen Guide

### BRIDGE

BRIDGE reviews planets such as Hatch, Cold, Heat, Wet, Low-G, Quar, Sanctuary, Market, Black, Gate, Nursery, and Clinic.

### JOBS

JOBS accepts transport, rescue, research, sanctuary, market, and loan contracts.

### HAB

HAB tends creature pens. Creature types include grazer, climber, wetling, quillox, glowcalf, frostfin, mire pup, and starling.

### ROUTE

ROUTE travels short, long, quarantine, storm, gate, and moon paths. Routes drain fuel, oxygen, feed, morale, and hull.

### MARKET

MARKET buys feed, med gel, oxygen, papers, habitat, and filter support.

### LOG

LOG tracks eggs, permits, sanctuary choices, black port dealings, rare buyers, and bonds.

## Core Loop

1. Review BRIDGE.
2. Accept a job.
3. Match creatures with HAB care.
4. Buy supplies at MARKET.
5. Pick ROUTE.
6. Read LOG and steer the ending path.

## Risk, Progress, And Endings

Low fuel, oxygen, feed, morale, or hull can ruin routes. Sanctuary and black-market choices shape reputation. Endings include sanctuary fleet, corp ranch, smuggler hold, discovery ark, creature riot, and stranded ship.

## Tips For First Run

- Buy feed before long routes.
- Keep hull and oxygen healthy.
- Use papers and habitat supplies before quarantine pressure.
- Sanctuary choices are safer but less profitable.
- Black port choices can pay, but they increase risk.

## Offline Fictional Scope

Creature transport, quarantine, inspections, black-market choices, space trade, and medical care are fictional offline game systems. This game does not provide real animal, veterinary, legal, trading, network, radio, or internet behavior.
