Genre: survival road drama.

## Overview

Tiny Wasteland is a compact road-survival campaign about moving west through broken regions. You manage food, water, medicine, fuel, scrap, morale, radiation, vehicle wear, party condition, and difficult choices.

## Goal Of A Run

Reach a viable ending before the road destroys the convoy. You can chase a safe-zone signal, build faction ties, found a camp, or simply keep enough people alive to matter.

## Controls

## Screen Guide

### ROAD

ROAD advances through regions such as Dust, Dry, Clinic, Orchard, Flats, Bones, Static, Bunker, Green, and Safe. Travel burns supplies and can raise radiation or vehicle wear.

### CAMP

CAMP is for rationing, treating, repairing, guarding, cooking, talking, mapping, staying quiet, checking signals, and keeping watch. Camp actions recover some stats at the cost of time or supplies.

### SCAV

SCAV sends the party into sites such as pharmacies, fuel stops, schools, depots, clinics, farms, garages, and towers. Higher-risk scavenging can find more loot, but it can add wounds, radiation, or morale trouble.

### PARTY

PARTY tracks survivor roles and condition. Scouts, medics, drivers, cooks, strangers, guards, teachers, and mechanics help shape event outcomes.

### TRADE

TRADE handles faction barter with groups like League, Baron, Medic, Scav, Camp, and Depot. Trade can convert spare goods into critical supplies.

### EVENT

EVENT presents storms, sickness, tribute, shortcuts, rescues, fog, signal choices, and raids. These are moral and survival decisions with lasting flags.

## Core Loop

1. Drive from ROAD until supplies or vehicle pressure rises.
2. Camp to recover morale, repair, or treat wounds.
3. Scavenge when you need supplies.
4. Trade when a faction can solve a shortage.
5. Make event choices that match the ending you want.

## Risk, Progress, And Endings

Food, water, fuel, and vehicle condition keep the run alive. Morale prevents collapse. Radiation and wounds create long-term danger. Endings include safe-zone, faction road, lone survivor, convoy dream, founded camp, signal trap, and collapse.

## Tips For First Run

- Water and fuel are early priorities.
- Repair before vehicle condition gets desperate.
- Scavenge safe sites first to learn the rhythm.
- Trade scrap when a missing resource would end the run.
- Do not chase every signal if the party is already weak.

## Offline Fictional Scope

Radio and signal references are fictional narrative events only. Tiny Wasteland does not detect real signals, access networks, scan, track, or provide survival guidance.
